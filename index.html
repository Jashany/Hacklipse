Q. What is Hacklipse?
Hacklipse is a hackathon organized by ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) Student Chapter Thapar. It's an event where participants come together to work collaboratively on software projects, solve problems, and showcase their coding skills.
Q. When and where is Hacklipse happening?
The Hackathon shall be conducted from 6th to 7th April 2024.
Q. Who can participate in Hacklipse?
  • The Hackathon is “OPEN TO ALL”.
